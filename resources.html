A short reading list

Books and references the Academy returns to.

These are the canonical olympiad references, many used by our coaches in their own training work, and several authored by them.

Olympiad training, general

  • problem solving Strategies, Arthur Engel The canonical reference for olympiad method.
  • Problems from the Book, Titu Andreescu & Gabriel Dospinescu Inequalities, algebra and number theory at depth.
  • Putnam and Beyond, Răzvan Gelca & Titu Andreescu A reference for the move from olympiad to university competition.
  • Mathematical Olympiad Treasures, Titu Andreescu & Bogdan Enescu Problems and solutions organised pedagogically by method.

By subject

  • Inequalities, Theorems, Techniques and Selected Problems, Zdravko Cvetkovski (Springer) By an Academy coach. The reference we use for olympiad inequalities.
  • Lectures on Mathematics: Selected Topics, Đorđe Baralić From the wider mathematical network represented by individual coaches.
  • Euclidean Geometry in Mathematical Olympiads, Evan Chen The modern standard for olympiad level synthetic geometry.
  • 104 Number Theory Problems, Titu Andreescu, Dorin Andrica & Zuming Feng A focused problem set with complete solutions.
